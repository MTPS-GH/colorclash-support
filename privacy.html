Privacy Policy — Color Clash: Arcade Last updated: April 21, 2026 Data Collection Color Clash: Arcade uses Google AdMob to display advertisements and Apple Game Center for leaderboards. App Tracking Transparency On first launch, Color Clash: Arcade uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework to ask whether you want to allow tracking for personalized advertising. If you select Allow, AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier to show you personalized ads. If you select Ask App Not to Track, AdMob will only show non-personalized ads (no device identifier is used for tracking). The game plays identically regardless of your choice. You can change your tracking preference anytime in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. AdMob AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve advertisements. Collection depends on your tracking choice above. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy. Game Center Game Center may collect your player ID and gameplay data (such as high scores) to enable leaderboard features. For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy. Data Stored On Your Device Your high score is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. No personal information is transmitted to Tim Smith or any server controlled by Tim Smith. Children's Privacy Color Clash: Arcade is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ATT prompt ensures that no tracking occurs without explicit user permission.
